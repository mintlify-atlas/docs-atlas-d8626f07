---
title: "Quickstart"
description: "Create your account, set up a group, and share your first track in under 5 minutes."
---

<Note>
  Snippet uses Google OAuth for authentication. You need a Google account to sign in — there is no email/password option.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in with Google">
    Navigate to `/login` on your Snippet instance. Click **Continue with Google** and complete the OAuth flow.

    After authenticating, you are redirected to `/dashboard` where you can see your groups and recently uploaded tracks.

    <Tip>
      If you received an invite link to join a group, click it before signing in. The invite link includes a redirect parameter that automatically adds you to the group after you authenticate.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your first group">
    From the dashboard, click **Create Group** in the top-right of the **Your Groups** section.

    Fill in the form:
    - **Group Name** (required) — a short name for your music circle, e.g. `Road Trip Playlist`
    - **Description** (optional) — describe what the group is for

    Click **Create Group** to confirm. Your new group appears on the dashboard immediately.

    <Info>
      The group is private by default. No one can find or join it without an invite link from you.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload your first track">
    Open your group by clicking it on the dashboard. Inside the group page, click **Upload Track**.

    In the upload dialog:
    1. Click **Select File** (or drag and drop) and choose an MP3 or MP4 file up to 50 MB.
    2. Enter a **Track Title** — the display name for the track.
    3. Enter the **Artist** name.
    4. Choose a **Group** from the dropdown (pre-selected if you opened the dialog from a group page).
    5. Click **Upload Track**.

    The file is uploaded to Supabase storage and the track metadata is saved to the database. Once complete, the track appears in the group's track list.

    ```
    Accepted formats: .mp3, .mp4 (audio/mpeg, audio/mp4, video/mp4)
    Maximum file size: 50 MB
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Play a track">
    Inside a group, click on any track to start playback. Tracks stream directly in your browser — no download required.

    To download a track to your device, use the download button on the track card.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Invite a friend">
    From your group page, click **Invite Members**.

    A dialog appears with a unique invite link for that group:

    ```
    https://your-domain.com/invite_user?group_id=<group-uuid>
    ```

    Click the copy button to copy the link to your clipboard, then share it with your friend via any channel (message, email, etc.).

    When your friend opens the link:
    - If they are already signed in, they are added to the group immediately.
    - If they are not signed in, they are redirected to the login page. After authenticating with Google, they are automatically added to the group and redirected to it.

    <Warning>
      Anyone with the invite link can join the group. Keep it private and do not post it publicly if you want to control membership.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>
