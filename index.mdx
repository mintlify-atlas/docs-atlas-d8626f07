## What is Snippet?

Snippet is a private music sharing platform that lets you upload MP3 files and share them with your friends through invite-only groups. Think of it as a personal music locker shared between a circle of friends — no public feeds, no algorithms, just music you want to share with the people you choose.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up with your email address. Snippet uses Supabase Auth for secure, passwordless login.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create or join a group">
    Create a new music sharing group and invite your friends, or accept an invitation to join an existing group.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload tracks">
    Upload MP3 files to a group. Add a title and artist name to keep your library organized.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Listen and share">
    Play tracks directly in the browser. Every member of the group can hear what's been shared.
  </Step>
</Steps>
